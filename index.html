Geobiology + Chemical Oceanography @ MIT-WHOI

I am a PhD student at MIT and WHOI. I am also part of the Harvard-MIT Health Sciences & Technology Graduate Education in Medical Sciences (GEMS) Training Program. Broadly, I aim to characterize the origin, evolution, and interactions of life and Earth. I explore life and Earth processes over long time scales and in the modern environment, both theoretically and experimentally, by borrowing tools from fields such as geochemistry, microbiology, and bioinformatics. My research interests are interdisciplinary, motivated by unanswered questions in astrobiology and dedicated to improving the human condition.
